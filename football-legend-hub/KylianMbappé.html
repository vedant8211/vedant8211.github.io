Kylian Mbappé – The Speed King

Full Name: Kylian Mbappé Lottin

Date of Birth: December 20, 1998

Country: France 🇫🇷

Position: Forward / Winger

Clubs: AS Monaco, Paris Saint-Germain (PSG), Real Madrid (2024–)

Career Highlights

  • FIFA World Cup Winner (2018) – scored in the final at age 19
  • World Cup Golden Boot Winner (2022) – 8 goals
  • Scored a hat-trick in the 2022 World Cup Final
  • Multiple-time Ligue 1 Champion with PSG
  • Most expensive teenager in football history
  • Transferred to Real Madrid in 2024

Playing Style

  • Lightning speed and acceleration
  • Deadly finishing with both feet
  • Excellent dribbling at high speed
  • Smart off-the-ball movement
  • Performs well under pressure

Quote

“Kylian is not just a great talent, he is already a world-class player.” – Thierry Henry

Fun Facts

  • Born in Bondy, a suburb of Paris
  • His idol is Cristiano Ronaldo
  • Speaks French, Spanish, and English
  • First teenager since Pelé to score in a World Cup final
  • Known for his signature folded arms celebration
  • A global football ambassador and youth role model
