🌟 Football Legends

Celebrating the greatest football players of all time — past, present, and future

📖 Player Biographies

1. Lionel Messi: Creative genius. Record Ballon d’Or winner. Key to Argentina’s 2022 World Cup win.

2. Cristiano Ronaldo: Legendary scorer. Played in top leagues. Known for discipline and athleticism.

3. Pelé: Brazilian icon with 3 World Cups. Over 1,000 goals. Changed the game forever.

4. Diego Maradona: Brilliant and bold. 1986 World Cup legend. One of the greatest dribblers ever.

5. Zinedine Zidane: Calm, classy midfielder. World Cup champion and elite coach.

6. Kylian Mbappé: Speed demon. Starred at the 2018 World Cup at just 19.

7. Lamine Yamal: Spanish prodigy. Skillful and smart far beyond his age.

8. George Best: A genius with flair. United’s most dazzling player of his era.

9. Bobby Charlton: 1966 World Cup winner. Leader and goal-scoring midfielder.

10. Kenny Dalglish: Scotland’s pride. Liverpool icon as both player and manager.

11. Ryan Giggs: Speed, skill, and longevity. United’s most decorated star.

12. Thierry Henry: Arsenal’s greatest. Graceful finisher and creative force.

13. Wayne Rooney: Tough, tenacious, and talented. Scored screamers and broke records.
