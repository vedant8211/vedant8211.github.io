Ryan Giggs

Full Name: Ryan Joseph Giggs

Date of Birth: November 29, 1973

Place of Birth: Cardiff, Wales

Position: Winger / Midfielder

Club: Manchester United (entire career)

National Team: Wales

Career Highlights

  • Won 13 Premier League titles
  • 2-time UEFA Champions League winner
  • 4 FA Cups and 3 League Cups
  • Made 963 appearances for Manchester United
  • Scored 168 goals in his club career

Legacy

  • One of the most decorated players in football history
  • Known for pace, dribbling, and consistency
  • Became interim manager of Manchester United in 2014
  • Later served as manager of the Wales national team
