Chhattisgarh is one of the most naturally rich, culturally vibrant, and historically significant states of India. Here's what makes it truly great:

Natural Beauty & Resources
  • Dense Forests: Over 44% of the state is covered in forests, rich in biodiversity.
  • Waterfalls: Home to majestic waterfalls like Chitrakote (called the Niagara Falls of India), Tirathgarh, and Amrit Dhara.
  • Rivers: Major rivers like Mahanadi, Indravati, Hasdeo, and Shivnath originate or flow through here.

Tribal Culture & Heritage
  • Around 30% of the population is tribal, preserving unique traditions, dance forms like Panthi, Raut Nacha, and art like Bastar Dhokra and Bell Metal Craft.
  • Rich in folk stories, forest-based lifestyles, and indigenous knowledge systems.
Historical & Spiritual Significance
  • Home to ancient temples like Bhoramdeo, Dongargarh, Rajim, and Sirpur (a significant Buddhist site).
  • Ancient Kalachuri dynasty influence, with archaeological richness.
Festivals & Fairs
  • Vibrant festivals like Bastar Dussehra, Madai, and Teeja show its deep cultural roots.
  • Bastar Dussehra is celebrated for 75 days, the longest festival in the world.
